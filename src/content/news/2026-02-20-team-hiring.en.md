## Looking for Someone Passionate About Fruit

We are rapidly expanding into international markets and need passionate, challenge-loving trade specialists to join our team. We offer competitive compensation, international exchange opportunities, and hands-on experience across the entire supply chain from farm to table.

### The Ideal Candidate

- Background in international trade or agriculture
- Strong English skills (Japanese is a plus)
- Comfortable working independently and in teams
- Genuine interest in fruits and agriculture

### How to Apply

Send your resume and cover letter to career@une-liaison.com with the subject line Trade Specialist Application. We will reach out to qualified candidates within one week of receiving your application.

Come join us in bringing the delicious taste of Taiwan fruits to the world!
