## The Team Behind the Record-Breaking Year

This year's record-breaking pineapple exports are the result of ideal weather conditions and the smart agriculture management systems that Taiwanese farmers have adopted in recent years. From soil testing and moisture monitoring to optimal harvest timing, every step is precisely managed through data analysis.

### The Importance of Local Partnerships

We deeply understand that maintaining consistent fruit quality requires building strong relationships with producing regions. Over the years, we have maintained long-term contracted partnerships with pineapple farmers in Chiayi, Tainan, and Pingtung.

### Future Outlook

Our next step is to expand Taiwan pineapples into Middle Eastern and European Union markets, while continuing to invest resources to help farmers upgrade their equipment and technology.
