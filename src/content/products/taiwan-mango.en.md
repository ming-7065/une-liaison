## Taiwan Mango: The Sweet Pride of Summer

Taiwan Irwin mango is renowned worldwide. The main season runs from May to July each year. Farmers carefully ripen each mango tree naturally on the branch, allowing the mangoes to fully accumulate sugars and fragrance.

### Distinctive Flavor

The fruit displays natural orange-yellow coloring with thin, smooth skin. At about 18-20 degrees sweetness, one bite delivers a rich tropical experience. The tender flesh has no fiber, melts in your mouth, leaving a lingering mango fragrance.

### Origin Story

The main producing areas are distributed in Tainan Yutin and Kaohsiung. Local farmers insist on sustainable cultivation without herbicides, growing in an eco-friendly manner so every mango absorbs the purest sunlight and moisture.

### Serving Suggestions

Choose fruits that are plump, skin without spots, and emit a natural fragrance. Chill before serving sliced, or make into mango juice or smoothies for a refreshing summer treat.
